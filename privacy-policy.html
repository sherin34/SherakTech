Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 16, 2026

At Sheraktech, we are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring the security of your personal information. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, and safeguard the information you provide to us, specifically when interacting with our Meta (Facebook/Instagram) Lead Ads.

1. Information We Collect

When you interact with our Meta Lead Ads or contact us through our website, we may collect the following information:

  • Personal Identification Information: Your Name.
  • Contact Information: Phone Number and Email Address.
  • Business Details: Company Name or Business Name.
  • Project Details: Information regarding the services you are inquiring about (Budget, Project Details).

2. How We Use Your Information

The information we collect is used strictly for the following purposes:

  • To contact you regarding your inquiry or quote request.
  • To provide and deliver the web development and e-commerce services you requested.
  • To communicate with you via phone, email, or WhatsApp regarding your project.
  • To improve our customer service and tailor our solutions to your business needs.

3. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We respect your privacy and do not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer your personal information to outside parties. Your data is kept confidential and is only accessible to authorized Sheraktech personnel who need it to assist you with your project.

4. Meta Ads and Third-Party Platforms

Information submitted through Meta Lead Ads is governed by this Privacy Policy as well as Meta's own privacy terms. We retrieve this information securely to get in touch with you about your web development needs.

5. Data Security

We implement a variety of security measures to maintain the safety of your personal information. Your data is stored securely and we take reasonable precautions to prevent unauthorized access or disclosure.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to request access to the personal information we hold about you, to request corrections to any inaccuracies, or to request the deletion of your data. If you wish to exercise any of these rights, please contact us.
